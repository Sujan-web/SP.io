A. “Good [morning/afternoon], Relative Network. This is SP speaking. How may I assist you today?”

B. “Could you please provide your company name, your name, and the device you are using?”

C. “Could you describe the issue you are experiencing? I’ll guide you through the resolution process.”

Customer's Part:

(Customer responds with their issue, device details, etc.)

2. Problem-Solving
Your Part:

Identify and address the issue based on the relevant problem type.

Customer's Part:

(Customer provides additional details or feedback on troubleshooting steps.)

3. Conclusion
Your Part:

A. “Can you confirm if the issue has been resolved? If not, I may need to escalate this to my team leader and will update you as soon as possible.”

B. “Is there anything else I can assist you with today?”

C. “Thank you for calling. Have a great day. Goodbye.”

Customer's Part:

(Customer confirms resolution or requests further assistance.)
